

## **Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct**

### **Our Pledge**

We pledge to make participation in our community harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience.
- We aim to foster an open, inclusive, and respectful environment.

### **Our Standards**

**Positive behavior includes:**

* Showing empathy and kindness
* Respecting differing opinions
* Giving and accepting constructive feedback
* Taking responsibility and learning from mistakes

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

* Use of sexualized language or imagery
* Personal attacks, trolling, or harassment
* Publishing private information without consent
* Any conduct considered unprofessional or unsafe

### **Enforcement**

- Community leaders are responsible for enforcing this Code.
- Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported via **GitHub Issues**.
- Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion and fairness.

### **Consequences**
1. **Warning** – Private notice with clarification and improvement guidance.
2. **Temporary Ban** – Restricted participation for repeated or serious offenses.
3. **Permanent Ban** – For severe or continued violations.
